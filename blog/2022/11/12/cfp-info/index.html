Call for Presentations Info Session

Join us for the SED call for presentations Info session on Thursday, Nov. 17th at 7pm ET to learn more about the CFP process for #SED23. SED planning team members, Karmel James and Patrick Connelly, will be available to provide an overview of the application process and answer your questions.The agenda will be as follows:

  • SED introductions
  • CFP process overview
  • Submissions
  • Evaluation criteria
  • Selection notification
  • Session preparation
  • First time speakers
  • Q&A
