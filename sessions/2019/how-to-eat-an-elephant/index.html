How to Eat An Elephant (& Maximize Pardot)

9:30 AM - 10:15 AM

Dahlonega

Marketing

For most marketers, there's a point almost always thirty days in to an implementation where they realize that Pardot can do everything! The joy of opportunity is quickly shadowed by massive Pardot ToDo lists that seem to grow everyday! And each item says "complete me today!" What is missing is a prioritized roadmap. After working with over 2,000 organizations, the common path of the most successful of them was distilled into a simple 10 step plan. By the end of this session, you will have a definitive number of where you're at, what your very next step is, and a clear understanding of how to completely maximize your Pardot org!

Casey Cheshire
