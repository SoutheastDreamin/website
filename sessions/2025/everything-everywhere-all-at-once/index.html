Everything, Everywhere, All at Once: A Career Talk for Those Who Want a Life With Room for It All

2:55 PM - 3:20 PM

Salon V

You enter the workforce and imagine your fulfilling future life—a great career, time to work out, dinner with friends, time with family. Perhaps you want to start a family one day, have kids, maybe three, maybe a dog. You want to find time for meditation, for community service—maybe continue your hobbies in art, music and hiking. And you want a great career—promotions and an executive title one day.Is it all possible? It is. This is about the “power of the why” and how to find your passion and purpose through the work you do, while having a great life outside of work. You can build a fulfilling career in corporate America. Join our panel who have done this for themselves and mentored countless others as they navigated career, family, self-care, and life. Hear their stories, their advice, and their honest reflections. Prepare to be inspired, motivated, and encouraged for a life with a career and space for it all.
