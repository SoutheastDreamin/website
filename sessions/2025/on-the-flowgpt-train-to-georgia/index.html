On the FlowGPT Train to Georgia

9:45 AM - 10:30 AM

Conference 2

If Gladys Knight were a process engineer instead of the Empress of Soul, you know she’d be using her imagination to craft AI prompts. With tools like FlowGPT, anyone can build Salesforce Flows without a developer background. But just because you can, does that mean you should—especially without a solid foundation?

This session is for everyone—whether you’re new to Flows and excited to learn, or an experienced process engineer looking to refine your skills and explore how to use FlowGPT more effectively.

What You’ll Learn:

  • For Beginners: Mastering the basics is key to building Flows that deliver real value.
  • For Experienced Users: Align automations with business goals and amplify your expertise with FlowGPT.

AI tools like FlowGPT can make you more efficient, but they can’t replace your deep understanding of your business and its users. We’ll show you how to pair that expertise with Salesforce Flow fundamentals to craft better FlowGPT prompts and build impactful automations.

Interactive Session Highlights:

  1. Use Case Exploration: Solve a relatable business problem.
  2. “Mad Libs” for Automation: Identify variables, refine requirements, and clarify inputs.
  3. Building Together: Craft and test a FlowGPT prompt live.
  4. Refinement and Success: Analyze results, refine the prompt, and create a solution that works.

Takeaways:

  • A take-home practical guide for discovery sessions, better prompts, and aligned automations.
  • Hands-on experience with FlowGPT to build smarter, more impactful solutions.
  • Confidence to take your FlowGPT skills to the next level and deliver meaningful results.

Whether you’re starting your journey or fine-tuning your skills, this session will help you keep your automations on track—just like that Midnight Train to Georgia.

Don’t miss this chance to learn, practice, and walk away with actionable insights. While we can’t promise Gladys Knight, we can promise soulful guidance for your FlowGPT success!
