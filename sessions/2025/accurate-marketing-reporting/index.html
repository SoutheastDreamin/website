5 Tips for Accurate Marketing Reporting in Salesforce

11:45 AM - 12:30 PM

Salon III & IV

Accurate reporting is essential for measuring the success of your marketing efforts, but it’s easy to overlook key details. In this session, we’ll share five essential tips to improve your marketing reporting in Salesforce. From setting up proper data tracking to aligning metrics with business goals, we’ll guide you through the best practices for building reports that provide actionable insights. Whether you’re new to Salesforce or looking to optimize your existing reports, these strategies will help you report correctly and drive better decision-making.
