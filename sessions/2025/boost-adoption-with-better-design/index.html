Boost Adoption with Better Design: UX Hacks for Admins

10:45 AM - 11:30 AM

Conference E

Struggling with low adoption and frustrated users? Good design is the missing piece! In this session, we’ll explore practical UX strategies that Salesforce Admins can use to create intuitive, user-friendly experiences—no design background required. By making small but impactful design tweaks, Admins can transform complex, cluttered, or confusing interfaces into seamless, efficient workflows that users actually enjoy.

We’ll cover:

  • Essential UX principles that make Salesforce easier to navigate
  • Common usability pitfalls in Salesforce and how to fix them
  • Quick-win design hacks that improve adoption and reduce support tickets
  • Real-world examples of Admin-led UX improvements that drive engagement

This session bridges the gap between design theory and Admin execution, offering actionable takeaways you can apply immediately. Whether you're looking to refine page layouts, optimize flows, or rethink record details, you'll leave with the confidence and tools to create a better Salesforce experience for your users.

Talia Johnson
