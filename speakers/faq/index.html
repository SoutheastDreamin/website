Onsite

Do I need to bring my own laptop?
Yes, you will need to bring your own device to present from. You will need to make sure that your device has HDMI out. The conference and the A/V company will not provide any necessary dongles.
Are my hotel room or travel expenses covered?
Unfortunately since this is community funded conference our budget does not have any allowences for these types of expenses for speakers.
How do I book my room?
Southeast Dreamin' has a block of hotel rooms provided by the venue for a discounted rate.
If I already purchased a Southeast Dreamin' ticket can I get a refund?
Yes! As a speaker, your entrance to the conference is covered. You can either reach out to Patrick Connelly in the speaker Slack or email registration@sedreamin.com
